NLP

Our natural language processing research extracts structure and signal from financial text at scale. We work on models that read earnings calls, news, filings, and macro commentary to support fundamental investment analysis.

Research Focus

  • Sentiment and topic modeling on earnings transcripts and corporate disclosures
  • Event detection and narrative tracking across global news and policy sources
  • Domain-adapted language models for financial terminology and context
  • Structured extraction from unstructured documents for downstream forecasting

Language is one of the richest sources of market information. Our NLP work helps turn that unstructured data into quantitative inputs for research and decision-making.
