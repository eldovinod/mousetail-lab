The Lab

Mousetail is a Leeds-based investment analysis lab dedicated to exploring the frontiers of machine learning and its applications in financial markets.

We combine academic rigor with practical engineering to create models that provide deep context for asset classes. Our work spans from foundational AI research in Safety, Vision, and NLP to practical applications in investment analysis.

Located in Leeds, we are part of a growing ecosystem of tech and finance, collaborating to advance the field of data-driven investment.
